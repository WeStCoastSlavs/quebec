Nantes

From the Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia that anyone can change

Nantes is a city in France on the Atlantic Ocean. It is at the south western end of Brittany. Nantes has always seen itself as belonging to the (historical) region of Brittany. At the moment, it is the capital of Loire Atlantique , which is not part of the (administrative) region of Brittany. Furthermore, the Gallo language was historically spoken more than Breton in Nantes. Contrary to Breton that is a Celtic language, Gallo is a roman language, derivated from the ancient languages of Oïl spoken in Northern France. Nantes has about 280,000 inhabitants and a metroplitan area of about 550,000 people. Jules Verne was a famous citizen of Nantes
