About me
My name is Hojjat. I was born in April 1983; I am a university student.

Before becoming an active user here, I was an Administrator on Persian Wikipedia. I was involved there a lot, but I retired in late August 2007, to spend more time on Simple English Wikipedia.

I am generally interested in cleanup tasks (like categorization), and those related to the Wikipedia community. Actually, what I praise about Wikipedia is its "community" and its "policies". I try to share my experience with RfDs and RfAs.

I am an administrator on a couple of other wikis, including Meta. I am a developer of MediaWiki software too. If you have difficulties in creating or changing a template, you can ask me for help.

You may check my edit count if you need, or check how much I've contributed as an administrator. I also gather some statistics myself. (They add to the information which is already available by Wikimedia itself.)

I also have an account on Wikimedia Toolserver and you can ask me for help if you need something to be done on the toolserver. I run HujiBot (changes) too.
