[change] Following Karl Marx?

The following passage

Then there was an October Revolution, a second revolution in Russia led by Communists who followed Karl Marx. The new government asked for peace from the Germans.

Is kind of misleading, in that it implies Karl Marx was the leader of the revolution. Perhaps this should be changed to Vladimir Lenin? 80.229.144.118 17:33, 10 July 2006 (UTC)
