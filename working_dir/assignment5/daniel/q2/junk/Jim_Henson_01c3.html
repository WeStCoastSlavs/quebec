Jim Henson

From the Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia that anyone can change

James Maury "Jim" Henson (September 24, 1936 – May 16, 1990) was an American artist, puppeteer, and filmmaker. He was best known for his work with the Muppets, which he helped to create.

Henson's best known puppet is Kermit the Frog, the host of The Muppet Show.
