Fribourg (canton)

Canton of Fribourg in Switzerland

Fribourg is one of the twenty-six cantons of Switzerland. About 250.000 people live in the canton. Of these, about 100.000 live in the urban area of the capital of this canton, Fribourg City.

[change] Geography

The canton of Fribourg lies in the west of Switzerland. The river Saane (Sarine) flows from the south to the north of the canton. It borders the cantons of Vaud, Bern and Neuchâtel.
