Stephen King

Stephen King (born September 21, 1947) is a famous writer from the U.S. state of Maine. He is known for writing scary books. Many of his stories have been made into movies as well.

His most famous books are the seven novels of the The Dark Tower series.[needs proving]

Here are some other famous books by Stephen King:

1974 - Carrie
1975 - 'Salem's Lot
1978 - The Stand
1979 - The Dead Zone
1981 - Cujo
1996 - The Green Mile
