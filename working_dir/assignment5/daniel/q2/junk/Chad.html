Chad

From the Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia that anyone can change

Flag of Chad

Chad is a country in Africa. The capital is N'Djamena. (A capital is the city where the government is.) It was owned by the French until 1960. It suffers from war,drought, Plague, and armed conflicts.
