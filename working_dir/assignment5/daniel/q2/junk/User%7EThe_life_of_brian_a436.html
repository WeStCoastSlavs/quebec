[change] Who am I?

Good Question. I'm a 16 year old Heavy metal fan from the suburbia of Munich, Germany (to be more precise the suburb of Ottobrunn). I'm currently at Year 10 of the local gymnasium (lycée, highschool whatever you want to call it) with emphasis on languages. My main interests are: Rock climbing, playing guitar, bass or piano, listening to (predominantely heavy metal) music, going to concerts, playing in bands and of course loitering out the front of the local supermarket or train station.....^^

Current favourite song: Sepultura- Refuse Resist
