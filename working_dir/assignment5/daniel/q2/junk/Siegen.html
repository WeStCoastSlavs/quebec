Siegen is a city in the German state North Rhine-Westphalia. It has about 110,000 inhabitants and a university.

[change] Twin Towns

It has been twinned with the borough of Spandau in Berlin since 1952. Other twin cities are:

  •  Netherlands, Rijnsburg, since 1963, continuation of partnership with amalgamated town of Katwijk as of 2006
  •  United Kingdom, Leeds, since 1966
  •  Belgium, Ypres, since 1967
  •  Poland, Zakopane, since 1989
  •  Germany,  Saxony, Plauen, since 1990
