Neuendorf-Sachsenbande is a municipality in Wilstermarsch, in the district of Steinburg, in Schleswig-Holstein, Germany. The municipality has an area of 19.31 km (12 mi). On 31 December 2006, the population was 493. The town is has the lowest point in Germany, which is 3.539 m (11.611 ft)below sea level.

[change] History

The lowest point in Germany. The small black line at the bottom of the placard marked "NN" is sea level.

The town was made on April 15, 2003, when the municipalities of Neuendorf bei Wilster, Sachsenbande, and Bredensee were all joined together.
