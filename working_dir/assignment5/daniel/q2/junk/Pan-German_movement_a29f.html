Pan Germania

Pan-German Movement (from Greek: pān, all, and Germania) is an ideological and political movement promoting the unification of all German-speaking peoples of Europe.

Its origins are to be found in the concept of predestination of Germany as leader of world. It supported in the economic politic of protectionism by Friedrich List, was tanned of racism by Joseph Arthur Gobineau, heritage of Hitler nazi ideology.
