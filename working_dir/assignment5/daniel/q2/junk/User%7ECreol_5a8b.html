Pet Projects

  • User:Creol/Dates - cleanup of the day and year pages to include articles we have (mainly birth and death listings) and remove the host of redlinks we may never create. Many of these pages fall into 3 categories: straight copy/pastes of the en:wp pages, partial copy/pastes (births for feb-june for example) or nearly blank. Years are easiest, the matching birth, death, movies, and books cats for that year supply most if not all info. Days requires seaching What links here pages to locate entries for the page.
