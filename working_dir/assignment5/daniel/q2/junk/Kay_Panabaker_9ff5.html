Stephanie Kay Panabaker (born May 2, 1990) is an American actress from Orange, Texas. Her perhaps best known roles are Emily on Life is Ruff and Debbie on Phil of the Future. She is a valedictorian and will soon go to college. Panabaker is the younger sister of actress Danielle. The two sisters starred together in the movie Read it and Weep for the Disney Channel. She has had recurring roles on the television series CSI:Crime Scene Investigations and Angel.
