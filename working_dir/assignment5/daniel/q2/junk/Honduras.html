Honduras

From the Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia that anyone can change

Honduras is a country in Central America. Its capital is Tegucigalpa. Most people of Honduras use the Spanish language. 7,483,763 people live in Honduras and it 112,492 kilometers in size. It is located right next to El Salvador. To one side is the caribian and to the other in the pacific ocean.
