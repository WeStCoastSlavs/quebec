Friedrichshain-Kreuzberg

From the Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia that anyone can change

The location of Friedrichshain-Kreuzberg in Berlin.

Friedrichshain-Kreuzberg is a borough of Berlin. It is a new borough, only made in 2001 by joining the old boroughs of Friedrichshain and Kreuzberg. It's area is 20.16 km² (7.8 sq mi) and has, as of 2003, about 255,200 inhabitants.

Its current mayor is Franz Schulz (Green Party).
