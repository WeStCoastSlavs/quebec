Worship

From the Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia that anyone can change

Worship is a word often used in religion. It means to have a lot of respect. If someone prays to God, people say that they worship God. Worship can also be used as the name for a church service or similar event. This can be called an act of worship.
