Saint Peter

From the Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia that anyone can change

A picture of Saint Peter

Peter, also called Simon (Cephas) Peter is one of the twelve apostles of Jesus. He is called a Saint by the Catholics. He is often talked about in the Bible.

It is not known when Peter was born. But the date of his death is in about the year 64. He died on the cross in Rome. Some sources say that Peter died head downwards. Others say he died head upwards.

Peter was married. The name of his wife is unknown nor is it known if he lived with her once he started his ministry.

Saint Peter was the first leader of an early Christian community for at least 34 years. At that time the term Pope or Papa was not yet coined. At that time there was only one Christian Church/Faith.

The supposed remains of St. Peter rest in Vatican City.
