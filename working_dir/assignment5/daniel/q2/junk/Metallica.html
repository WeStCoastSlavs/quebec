Metallica

From the Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia that anyone can change

A Metallica concert in London

Metallica is an American thrash metal/heavy metal band. They formed in Los Angeles in 1981 and helped to invent the Thrash sub genre of heavy metal. Since then they have become one of metal's most popular and successful bands, and have sold over 100 million CDs worldwide. The bands fifth album has sold over fourteen million copies, making it the twenty fifth biggest selling album of all time in America.
