Aachen is a German city in the state of North Rhine-Westphalia. It has about 260,000 inhabitants and a well-known university, the Rheinisch-Westfälisch Technische Hochschule (RWTH) and a bright history starting at the beginning of the middle ages.

Since 1972, Aachen has been part of the Cologne Government Region.
