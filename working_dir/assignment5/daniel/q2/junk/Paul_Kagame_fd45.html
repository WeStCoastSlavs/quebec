Paul Kagame

From the Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia that anyone can change

Paul Kagame (born October 23, 1957) is the President of Rwanda. Though a member of the Tutsi ethnic group, he tends to downplay the importance of his ethicity. He is responsible for ending the Rwandan Genocide. However, he is often seen as a dictator, and has a bad human rights record.
