Gießen

Map of Hesse highlighting the Regierungsbezirk of Gießen
State Hesse
District seat Gießen
Area 5,381.14 km²
Population 1,061,444 (30 Sep. 2005)
Pop. density 197 /km²

Gießen is one of the three Regierungsbezirke of Hesse, Germany. It is in the middle of the state.
