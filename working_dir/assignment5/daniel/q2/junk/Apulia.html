Apulia is a region in south Italy on the Adriatic Sea. The capital is Bari. The population was about 4.071.518 in 2004.

[change] Provinces

  • Bari
  • Brindisi
  • Foggia
  • Lecce
  • Taranto

In the year 2009 there will another province:

  • Barletta-Andria-Trani
