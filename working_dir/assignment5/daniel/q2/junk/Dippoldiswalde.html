Dippoldiswalde is a town in the Free State of Saxony, Germany. It is the capital city of the Weißeritzkreis district. It is situated 23 km (14 mi) east of Freiberg, and 18 km (11 mi) south of Dresden.

The town is situated on the Weisseritz railway, a narrow gauge railway powered by steam locomotives.
