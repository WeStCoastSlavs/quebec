Francis of Assisi

From the Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia that anyone can change

St. Francis as painted by El Greco

Saint Francis of Assisi ( september 26th 1181-October 3, 1226) was a Christian religious figure and Saint. He is the founder of the Franciscan Order and patron saint of animals, the environment, merchants, Italy and Catholic Action. He was born in Assisi in Italy where a large basilica was built in his honour.

On his feast day, October 4, many churches have a ceremony for people to bring their pets and working animals to church.
