Category:People from former German territories

From the Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia that anyone can change

this category is for people who were born in places which are not now part of the Federal Republic of Germany, but were once part of Germany in Europe, eg Ernst Reuter, born in a part of Prussia which is now Denmark or Günther Grass, born in Danzig, which is now Gdansk, Poland.
