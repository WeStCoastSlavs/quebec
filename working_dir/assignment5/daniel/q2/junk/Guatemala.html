Guatemala

From the Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia that anyone can change

The flag of Guatemala.

Guatemala is a country in Central America.

[change] Population

It is the country with the largest population in Central America, and its neighbors are the countries Belize, Mexico, El Salvador and Honduras. More than fourteen million people live in the 22 Guatemalan departments.

[change] Trivia

Guatemala is famous in the world for its natural beauty, and is one of the favorite tourist destinations in the world.
