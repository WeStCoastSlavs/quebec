Hello and welcome to my talk page! I am Claimgoal/Necknoise/Flamethrower and my real name is Joseph. I am a Filipino and I am nine years old. I live in Australia (and just so you know I speak the Australian accent!). I had heard about Wikipedia in 2006 and made an account in 2008 (both English Wikipedia and Simple English). I still contributed in 2006 & 2007. I have an English Wikipedia account as well but with my old name (Claimgoal). On Saturdays and Thursdays I go to Karate lessons. I am not Flamethrower from EN wiki. In my free time I pick one of the following choices:

  • Go on Wikipedia
  • Play fighting games such as Tekken and Soulcalibur.
  • Watch TV.
  • Practice Karate moves I know.
  • Punch the giant stress ball I have at home.
  • Homework (which I hate but I have to do when I have any)
  • Exercise
  • Watch videos on YouTube.
  • Eat.

If I have done anything wrong I apologize. In April 2008, Creol changed my name to "Necknoise" since I requested it. I got the idea from a captcha test just like I did with my old user name but used two halves of two tests. This one was inspired by a captcha test. Sometimes my status is wrong so don't ALWAYS rely on it. May 2008 Archer7 changed my name to Flamethrower but it will be moved to Choco instead because Flamethrower is already taken on EN wiki.

Because of school, Chocolates will not be very active on weekdays, but should be back editing voraciously on weekends (except while doing homework).
