Elbe

From the Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia that anyone can change

The Elbe River (Czech Labe  listen (info • help), Sorbian/Lusatian Łobjo, Polish Łaba, German Elbe) is one of the major waterways of central Europe. It comes from the North West Czech Republic. Then it goes through much of Germany and finally it flows into the North Sea.
