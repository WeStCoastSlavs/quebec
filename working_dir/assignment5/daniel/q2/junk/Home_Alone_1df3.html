Home Alone

From the Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia that anyone can change

Home Alone is a 1990 comedy movie. It was written and produced by John Hughes and directed by Chris Columbus. It stars Macaulay Culkin as Kevin McCallister, a boy who is left behind by his family when they go on a vacation to France, over Christmas. While his family is gone, Kevin must stop two burglars trying to rob his house. The film was very popular and three sequels were made.
