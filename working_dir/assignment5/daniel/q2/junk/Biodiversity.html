Biodiversity

From the Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia that anyone can change

Rainforests are among the most biodiverse ecosystems on earth

Biodiversity or biological diversity is the variation of different life forms within a given ecosystem or for the entire Earth. Biodiversity is often a measure of the health of biological systems to indicate if there could be a danger that species get extinct. Variation helps species live in a population, therefore creating Biodiversity.
