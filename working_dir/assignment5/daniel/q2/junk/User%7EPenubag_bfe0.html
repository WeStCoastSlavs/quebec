Hello, I mostly edit on the English Wikipedia, however, I will sometimes lurk over to the Simple English Wikipedia. My only purpose on the Simple English is to write articles, that means I won't participate in community discussions or vandalism patrolling. Whenever I start a Simple English article, I will almost always finish it, fleshed out and completed. If you feel a need to contact me, you may put it on my Talk page or e-mail me.

[change] Notes

One of my main goals as an en.wikipedia user is to get the Simple English more recognized. My image to the left is what I think the ideal way of accomplishing this would be (see the sidebar under the toolbox), here are other versions I have made: lowercased, bigger notifier, a template rather than a sidebar (see bottom), although the one to the left is my favorite. I proposed this to the community but no one else seemed interested and seemed like they did not really care. I constantly tried asking and telling them that this was a good idea, however they are all indifferent.

My rationale for this proposal: (or you can view this)
Learning at a young age is important, and understanding what you are learning is the most important part, that is why the Simple English Wikipedia is crucial. All Simple English articles are written in vain because the Simple English link is hidden away in the depths of the interwikis, how can any kid find or even know of the Simple English's existence? That is a lot of wasted time for simple English editors who worked hard to get these articles up; and the articles want to be read! Hopefully I can bring this proposal to people's attention.
