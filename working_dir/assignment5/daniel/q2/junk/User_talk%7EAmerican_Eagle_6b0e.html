[change] Birds

American Eagle, I have just reverted your changes. The word "like" was used correctly in every single instance where you changed it. I suppose you did it to avoid confusion with the use of the word as a verb to indicate a positive feeling eg "I like icecream".

The word "like" is correctly use as a conjunction, forming a " simile" (saying that two things are similar). Many of your changes made absolute nonsense of the sense, or else resulted in a statement which was simply false!

"Though birds are warm-blooded creatures such as mammals, they do not give birth to live babies." Nonsense!

This is one of your changes which has resulted in a ridiculous inaccuracy. Birds are not warm-blooded creatures such as mammals. This makes mammals a sub-category of Birds! Birds are warm-blooded creatures like mammals.

let me say once again that this use of the word "like" is a simple, old, correct English use. If you have mmade similar changes to other web pages, then please revert them, because it is creating chaos.
