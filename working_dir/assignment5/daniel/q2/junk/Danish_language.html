Danish language

From the Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia that anyone can change

Danish dialects

Danish is the Germanic language spoken in Denmark, the Faroe Islands, and parts of Greenland and Germany (Southern Schleswig). Also Scanian can considered to be an East-Danish dialect group. To the Danish people, or Danes, the language is known as Dansk.
