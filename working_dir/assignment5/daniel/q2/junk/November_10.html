November 10 is the 315th day of the year (316th in leap years) in the Gregorian calendar. There are 50 days remaining until the end of the year.

[change] Events

  • 1444 - Battle of Varna. King Ladislas of Poland and Hungary is killed.
  • 1775 - United States Marine Corps was created
  • 1938 - The Kristallnacht ends. About 30 000 Jews were moved to concentration camps, and over 1500 synagogues were partly destroyed
  • 1975 - The SS Edmund Fitzgerald sinks

[change] Births

  • 1483 - Martin Luther, German Protestant reformer (d. 1546)
  • 1668 - François Couperin, French composer (d. 1733)
  • 1683 - George II, German-born King of Britain (d. 1760)
  • 1759 - Friedrich von Schiller, German writer (d. 1805)
  • 1928 - Ennio Morricone, Italian composer
  • 1944 - Sir Tim Rice, English lyricist
  • 1959 - Nancy Cartwright, American actress
  • 1969 - Jens Lehmann, German footballer
  • 1977 - Brittany Murphy, American actress

[change] Deaths

  • 1549 - Pope Paul III, last of the Renaissance popes (b.1468)
  • 1916 - Walter Sutton, American biologist (b. 1877)
  • 1938 - Mustafa Kemal Atatürk, first President of Turkey (b. 1881)
  • 1982 - Leonid Brezhnev, leader of the Soviet Union (b.1906)
