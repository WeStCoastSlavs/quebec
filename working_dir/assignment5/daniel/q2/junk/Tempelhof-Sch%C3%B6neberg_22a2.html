Tempelhof-Schöneberg

From the Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia that anyone can change

The location of Tempelhof-Schöneberg in Berlin.

Tempelhof-Schöneberg is one of the new borough of Berlin, formed in 2001 by merging the old boroughs of Tempelhof and Schöneberg.

Tempelhof-Schöneberg has an area of 53.09 km² (20.5 sq mi) and has, as of November 2004, about 335.000 inhabitants. Its current mayor is Ekkehard Band (SPD).
