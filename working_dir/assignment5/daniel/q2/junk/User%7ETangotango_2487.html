Contact

My aim is, and has always been, to make fair edits that benefit the encyclopedia as a whole. I will occasionally make mistakes; if you notice, please feel free to leave me a message on my talk page.

I am an administrator here, on the English Wikipedia, and on the Wikimedia Meta-Wiki. If you need any assistance, please don't hesitate to ask. For more information about me, please see my user page on the English Wikipedia or the one on Meta.

You may be interested in my toolserver tools. They can be found at http://tools.wikimedia.de/~tangotango/.
