Sardinia is a region in the center of Italy. The capital is Cagliari. The population was about 1,655,677 in 2005.

[change] Provinces

  • Cagliari
  • Carbonia-Iglesias
  • Medio Campidano
  • Nuoro
  • Ogliastra
  • Olbia-Tempio
  • Oristano
  • Sassari
