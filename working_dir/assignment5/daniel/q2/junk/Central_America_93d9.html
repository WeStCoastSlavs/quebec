Central America

From the Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia that anyone can change

Central America is a region between Guatemala and Belize in the North, and Colombia at the South.

Central America in red

The countries in Central America, from north to south are:

  • Guatemala
  • Belize
  • Honduras
  • El Salvador
  • Nicaragua
  • Costa Rica
  • Panama

They are all Spanish speaking countries, apart from Belize, whose people also speak English.

Central America is part of the continent known as North America.
