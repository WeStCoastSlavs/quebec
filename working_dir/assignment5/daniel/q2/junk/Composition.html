Composition

From the Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia that anyone can change

Composition means literally putting together.

The word "composition" is used in literature, drama and poetry to refer to the way that words are put together.

Musical composition means putting notes together to make a piece of music.

The word "composition" is used in art to mean the way that colours or shapes are put together.

It is also used in mathematics and science in a number of ways, all referring to "putting things together".
