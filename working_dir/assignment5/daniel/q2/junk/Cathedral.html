Cathedral

From the Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia that anyone can change

A cathedral is a large Christian church. It is the seat of a Bishop.

The 'Ulmer Münster' (Cathedral of Ulm) is a Lutheran Cathedral. It is the tallest church (at 162m spire) and the biggest Protestant church in the world.

Basilica of Our Lady of Peace of Yamoussoukro, also known as Basilique de Notre Dame de la Paix de Yamoussoukro, Côte d'Ivoire. It is supposedly the largest church in the world.

Hohe Domkirche St.Peter and Maria, is the Cathedral of Cologne, Germany. It is a UNESCO World Heritage site. It is also one of the most famous churches in Germany.

The dome (cathedral) of Speyer is the biggest romanesque Church in the world that still stands. The biggest romanesque church used to be the abbey of Cluny. The abbey was destroyed and now lies in ruins.
