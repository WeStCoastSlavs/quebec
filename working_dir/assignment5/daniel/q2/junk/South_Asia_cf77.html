South Asia

From the Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia that anyone can change

South Asia (or Southern Asia) is the south area of Asia. There are 13 countries of this region. Around it is (from west to east) West Asia, Central Asia, Eastern Asia, and Southeastern Asia.

It includes these countries:

  • Myanmar (Burma)
  • Bangladesh (East Bengal)
  • Tibet (including South Tibet, China)
  • Sikkim (part of the Republic of India)
  • Bhutan
  • Hindustan (Bharat)
  • Maldives
  • Nepal
  • Khalistan (East Punjab, Haryana and Himachal Pradesh)
  • Pakistan and Indian Occupied Kashmir
  • Sri Lanka

The United Nations also includes Iran and Afghanistan.
