Neustadt an der Weinstraße is a city located in Rhineland-Palatinate, Germany. It has about 54,000 people.

[change] Districts

Its districts are

  • Diedesfeld,
  • Duttweiler,
  • Geinsheim,
  • Gimmeldingen,
  • Haardt,
  • Hambach,
  • Königsbach,
  • Lachen-Speyerdorf,
  • Mußbach, and
  • Winzingen.
