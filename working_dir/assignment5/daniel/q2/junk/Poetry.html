Poetry

From the Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia that anyone can change

Poetry is a type of art form and a type of literature. Poetry uses the qualities of words, including their length and sound, as well as the meanings of the words to be artistic. Poetry can take many forms (haiku, thematic, nursery rhymes), and is used to describe many things (comparing, talking about, expressing emotion). It can make sense or be nonsense, it can rhyme or not, and it can be as small as a few words, or as long as a book (an epic). It takes many shapes and sizes, it can be serious, or it can be funny.

"To say something poetically" means to give information in an artistic way.

Poetry has different uses of tone. There are many forms of poetry such as Haiku, Ballad, and Ode. Others are Free verse and Blank verse.
