November 4 is the 309th day of the year (310th in leap years) in the Gregorian calendar. There are 56 days remaining until the end of the year.

[change] Events

  • 2005 - Nintendo's Game Boy Micro released in Europe.
  • 2040 - Partial solar eclipse

[change] Births

  • 1470 - Edward V, King of England (d. 1483?)
  • 1916 - Walter Cronkite American news reporter
  • 1946 - Laura Bush, American First Lady, Wife of George W. Bush
  • 1948 - Amadou Toumani Touré, President of Mali
  • 1961 - Ralph Macchio, American actor
  • 1985 - Marcell Jansen, German footballer

[change] Deaths

  • 1847 - Felix Mendelssohn, German composer (b. 1809)
  • 1924 - Gabriel Fauré, French composer (b. 1845
  • 1955 - Cy Young, American baseball player (b. 1867)
