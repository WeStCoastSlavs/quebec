Emilia-Romagna is a region in the North-east Italy on the Adriatic Sea. The capital is Bologna. The population was about 4,151,369 in 2004.

  • Bologna

  • Forlì-Cesena
  • Modena
  • Parma
  • Piacenza
  • Ravenna
  • Reggio Emilia
  • Rimini
