[change] Events

  • January 4 - The United States Mint issues the first of their 50 state quarters.
  • March to June - Kosovo War
  • April 1 - Nunavut becomes Canada's third territory, under the control of the Inuit people.
  • April 20 - Two Littleton, Colorado teenagers (Eris Harris and Dylan Klebold) kill twelve of their fellow students, one teacher, and then themselves. This is known as the Columbine High School massacre.
  • December 31 - Control over the Panama Canal goes back to Panama.
