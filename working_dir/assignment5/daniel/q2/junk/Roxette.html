Roxette

From the Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia that anyone can change

Roxette was a Swedish pop music group formed in 1986. The band had only 2 members, Per Gessle and Marie Fredriksson. Their song "It Must Have Been Love" was one of the most sold singles in EMI's catalogue. It was released first as a Christmas song in 1987 and then as an "ordinary song" in 1990. In 1991, Roxette release the smashing album "Joyride", which made them to go on tour worldwide, playing 107 concerts.
