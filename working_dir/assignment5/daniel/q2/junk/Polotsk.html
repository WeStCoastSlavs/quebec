Polotsk

From the Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia that anyone can change

Polotsk[1][2][3][4] (Polatsk, Belarusian: По́лацк, Russian: Полоцк, Polish: Połock) is a historical city in Belarus, located on the Dvina river. It is the center of Polotsk district in Vitsebsk Voblast. The population is about 79,000. It is served by Polotsk Airport and during the Cold War was home to Borovitsy air base.
