Salzburg is the capital of the Austrian state of Salzburg. The population is about 150,000. Its most famous citizen was Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart. Some other well-known composers that came from the area are Antonio Caldara, Joseph Hoelzl, and Rupert Unterholtzer.
