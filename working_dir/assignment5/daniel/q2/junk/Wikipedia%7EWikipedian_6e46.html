Wikipedia:Wikipedian

From the Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia that anyone can change

A Wikipedian is a person who helps to contribute to Wikipedia. For example, a user is a Wikipedian. Wikipedians are people who are part of the Wikipedia community and edit at times.
