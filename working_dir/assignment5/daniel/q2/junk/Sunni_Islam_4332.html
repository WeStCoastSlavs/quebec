Sunni Islam

From the Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia that anyone can change

Sunni Muslims are the largest denomination of the Islamic religion. They are referred to as Ahl ul-Sunna (Arabic: أهل السنة; "people of the tradition"). The word Sunni comes from the word sunna (Arabic : سنة ), which means the tradition of the Prophet of Islam, Muhammad. Sunnis are also called Ahlu s-Sunna wa-l-Jama'ah (Arabic: أهل السنة والجماعة) (people of tradition and congregation) which means that the Sunnis are united. They are the branch of Islam that came through the caliphate, which started with Abu Bakr.
