DodekBot


[change] Bot

DodekBot is Dodek's interwiki bot.

This user account is a bot operated by Dodek (talk).

It is not a sock puppet (impersonator), but rather an automated or semi-automated account for making repetitive edits that would be extremely tedious to do manually.
