Rotterdam

From the Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia that anyone can change

Rotterdam is the second largest city in the Netherlands. About 588,000 people live there. The biggest city is Amsterdam. In the urban area, there are about 1.1 million people.

Rotterdam also has one of the biggest, and busiest ports in the world. It is the biggest port in Europe. The ports of Hamburg and possibly Marseille have a similar size.
