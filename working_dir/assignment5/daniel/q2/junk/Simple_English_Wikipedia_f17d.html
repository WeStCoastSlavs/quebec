Simple English Wikipedia

From the Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia that anyone can change

The official Simple English Wikipedia logo

Simple English Wikipedia is an English edition of Wikipedia, written in simple English. It was made in 2003. On July 26, 2006, the Simple English Wikipedia had 10,000 articles. As of June 22, 2008, it has more than 30,000 articles, over 80,000 pages and over 19,000 registered users.
