Federation

From the Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia that anyone can change

A federation is a union of a number of self-governing states or regions, which are all ordered by a central governemnt.

United States of America is a federation. The federal (central) government has the highest executive role, but the states have their own special rules in some specal cases.
