Sigourney Weaver (born as Susan Alexandra Weaver on October 8, 1949 in New York City) is an American actress.

She played the role of Lieutenant Ellen Ripley in the movie series Alien, existing from the 4 movies Alien, Aliens, Alien³, and Alien: Resurrection. She is also well known for playing Dana Barrett in the movies Ghostbusters and Ghostbusters II or for the role Dian Fossey in the movie Gorillas in the Mist.
