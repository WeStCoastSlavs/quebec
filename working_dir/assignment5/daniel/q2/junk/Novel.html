Novel

From the Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia that anyone can change

A novel is a long work of literature that is fiction. Novels in English are usually 50,000 words or more, and have 100 pages or more.

[change] Genres of novels

There are many different genres, or categories, of novels. Some of these are:

  • Campus
  • Crime fiction
  • Fantasy
  • Gothic
  • Horror
  • Romance
  • Spy
  • Thriller
  • Science fiction
  • Speculative
  • Westerns
