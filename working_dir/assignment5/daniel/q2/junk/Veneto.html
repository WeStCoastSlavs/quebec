Veneto is a region in North-east Italy on the Adriatic Sea. The capital is Venice. The population was about 4,699,950 in 2004.

[change] Provinces

  • Venice (Venezia) (capital) or better in Italian "Capoluogo di regione"
  • Belluno
  • Padua (Padova)
  • Rovigo
  • Treviso
  • Verona
  • Vicenza
