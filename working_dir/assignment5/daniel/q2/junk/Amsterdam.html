Amsterdam

From the Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia that anyone can change

Location of Amsterdam

Amsterdam is the capital and largest city in the European country of the Netherlands. Amsterdam is famous for its canals and dikes. Unlike most other countries, the government is not in Amsterdam, but in The Hague.

Amsterdam has about 800,000 inhabitants, two universities and an international airport "Schiphol Airport". About 1.5 miilion people live in the metropolitan area.

Marijuana usage, magic mushrooms usage, same sex marriages, prostitution and euthanasia are legal here.
