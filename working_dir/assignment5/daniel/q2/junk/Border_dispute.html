Border dispute

From the Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia that anyone can change

A border dispute is when two countries or more do not agree where the border is between the two countries, such as State of Israel and Palestine and Pakistan and the Republic of India over disputed territory of Kashmir.

Wars are sometimes fought over border disputes.
