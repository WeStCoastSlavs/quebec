The Arc de Triomphe in Paris

The Arc de Triomphe, in the end of the Champs-Elysées, is a very famous monument in Paris.

It was commissioned in 1806 after the victory at Austerlitz by Emperor Napoléon Bonaparte. it is a large arc, and even though it may be thought by some people that you can drive underneath it, you cannot.
