Swaziland

From the Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia that anyone can change

Swaziland is a country in Africa. Its capital is Mbabane. It is completely surrounded by land. Swaziland doesn't touch the sea. The two countries that touch it are Mozambique and South Africa.
