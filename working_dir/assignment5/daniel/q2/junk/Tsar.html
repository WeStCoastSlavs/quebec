Tsar

From the Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia that anyone can change

Tsar is a name for the ruler of Bulgaria in 913–1422, later usage in 1908–1946 and the emperor of Russia from 1546 until 1917. It is also spelled Tzar, Czar, and Csar, and is the EasternEuropean word for Caesar.

The last Tsar to Rule Russia was Tsar Nicholas II
