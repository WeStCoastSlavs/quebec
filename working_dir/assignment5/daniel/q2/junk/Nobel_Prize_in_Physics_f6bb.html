Nobel Prize in Physics

From the Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia that anyone can change

The Nobel Prize in Physics is a Nobel Prize in the science of Physics.

Some famous winners are:

  • Albert Einstein
  • Werner Heisenberg
  • Marie Curie
