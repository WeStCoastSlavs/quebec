Mount McKinley (Denali) in Alaska (USA).

Mount McKinley or Denali is the highest mountain peak in North America. It is about 20,320 feet(6,193.6 m) high[1]. It is located in the main part of the Alaska Range. It is also called Denali which means "the high one" in Athabaskan language.

[change] Location

Mount McKinley is located in the central portion of the Alaska Range. It is about 130 miles (209 km) north-northwest of Anchorage and 155 miles (249 km) southwest of Fairbanks. The summit is about 35 miles (56 km) from the nearest major road, the George Parks Highway.
