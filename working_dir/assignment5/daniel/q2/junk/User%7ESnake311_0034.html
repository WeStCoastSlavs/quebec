How to become a sysop

  • Note: This information here isn't official and dosen't have to do any thing in the simplewiki.

Requirements

  • A user must have more than 1000 edits in the simplewiki and at least 90 days of full experience to be a sysop.
  • For a user to become a sysop, he/she must have over 66% of all votes that support the nomiator.
  • A user must be familar with the rules on simplewiki and guidelines on the Meta-Wiki to be a sysop.

Sysop article draft

  • "A user that wishes to be an administrator in the Simple Wikipedia, (a) must have a minimum experience of 4 to 5 months, (b) must have a minimum of 500+ number of edits, (c) and must have proved to be trusted on handaling admin tasks (example; reverting vandalism, greeting and helping other users, defending the wiki from vandals and users who repeatedly despited warned, give bad-faith edits, etc.,). In addition, he or she must qualify for at least two of these expected criteria in order to be an administrator and all candidates must have at least 2/3 of votes that says "support"." - Orginial copy
  • "A user that wishes to be an administrator in the Simple Wikipedia, (a) must have a minimum experience of 90 days, (b) must have a minimum of 800+ number of edits, (c) and must have proved to be trusted on handaling admin tasks (example; reverting vandalism, greeting and helping other users, defending the wiki from vandals and users who repeatedly despited warned, give bad-faith edits, etc.,). In addition, he or she must qualify for at least two of these expected criteria in order to be an administrator and all candidates must have at least 2/3 of votes that says "support"." - Shortned experience time & increase contributations count
