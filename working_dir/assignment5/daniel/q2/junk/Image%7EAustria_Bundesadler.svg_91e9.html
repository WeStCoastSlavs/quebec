This media file needs to be cleaned up, because: the coats of arms of Austria from 1919-1934 were without the broken chains, which were added after 1945 following the liberation from Nazi Germany. Please create a coa for this period of the First Republic and name the image "Austria Bundesadler 1919-1934" or something like that. It also could be that the legs were slightly lower as a result, see Image:Coats of arms of Austria (1919 - 1934).jpg but this should be verified first somehow. thanks.
