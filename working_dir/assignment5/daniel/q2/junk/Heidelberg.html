Heidelberg in Germany
Heidelberg. View of city, bridge and castle

Heidelberg is a city by the Neckar river in the south-west of Germany in the state of Baden-Württemberg. It has about 145,000 inhabitants and a well-known university. The old town and the castle of Heidelberg are well-known tourist attractions of Germany.
