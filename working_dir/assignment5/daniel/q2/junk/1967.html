  • April 28 - Montreal hosts Expo '67.
  • May 1 - Elvis Presley married Priscilla Beaulieu.
  • June 5 - June 10 - The Six-Day War
  • December 3 - Dr. Christiaan Barnard transplanted the first heart.
  • The Summer of Love
  • The Blue Oyster Cult forms.
  • Jethro Tull forms.
