[change] Very good article process

The English Wikipedia have something they call featured article. Featured articles were chosen by some editors. These editors think that the articles chosen are in fact very good articles. They are well,-written, or explain a concept very adeptly.

I have come up with a similar idea.

The criteria are here. Discussion about the criteria is possible. Original postings to simple talk are here and here.

The basic idea was that out of the 15.000 articles present, 1% (150 articles) should be very good ones. Currently, there are about 8 articles up for discussion here.
