Travel

From the Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia that anyone can change

Travel is the activity going from one place to another location. When a person makes the same trip every day to work or school, this type of travelling is usually called "commuting." Some people travel to other cities as part of their job. This is called "business travel." Some people travel on their vacation, to visit other towns, cities, or countries. Some people write about traveling in books called autobiographies or journals.

Types of travelling:

  • Hiking
  • Cruise ship vacations
  • Train journeys
  • Hitch-hiking
  • Driving
