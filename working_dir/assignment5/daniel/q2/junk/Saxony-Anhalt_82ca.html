Saxony-Anhalt is a Bundesland (state) in Germany. It has an area of 20,445.26 km² (7,894 sq mi). 2,580,626 people live there. The capital is Magdeburg.

Big cities in Saxony-Anhalt are:

  • Dessau
  • Halberstadt
  • Halle (Saale)
  • Wittenberg
  • Magdeburg (capital)
  • Merseburg
  • Naumburg (Saale)
  • Quedlinburg
  • Stendal
  • Weißenfels
