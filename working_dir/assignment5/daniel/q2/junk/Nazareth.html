Nazareth

From the Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia that anyone can change

Nazareth is the capital city of the north district of Israel. Most of the people in this city are Arabs (who are both Muslim and Christian) - as well as some Jewish people. The city is very old and is also famous for being the city where it is believed Jesus grew up.
