Opposite

From the Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia that anyone can change

Here are some

opposite words:
hot cold
left right
up down
near far
small big
in out
slow fast
dry wet

Opposite means that one thing is completely different from another. It might also mean that only one part is different.

Opposite also means personality, reverse simple things such big small and left and right, or mirror.
