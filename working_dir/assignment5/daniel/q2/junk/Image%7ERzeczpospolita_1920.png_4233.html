Early version of my map of Poland and Eastern Europe between 1918 and 1945. So far it features the borders and major towns and cities, as well as the frontlines of the Polish-Bolshevik War.
The colours and fonts and other stuff will be added soon.

Based on my maps of the Polish-Lithuanian Commonwealth series, colours stolen from some German map printed in 1938
