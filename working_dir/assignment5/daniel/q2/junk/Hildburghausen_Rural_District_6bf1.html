Hildburghausen is a district in Thuringia, Germany. It covers most of the same territory as the old Ernestine duchy of Saxe-Hildburghausen.

[change] Coat of arms

The coat of arms displays:

  • the heraldic lion of Meißen
  • the cock representing the counts of Henneberg, who ruled the region until 1583
  • below the symbol of the bishopric of Würzburg is displayed
