Szczecin Lagoon

From the Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia that anyone can change

The Lagoon of the Oder River is an inland water basin situated in the southwestern part of the Baltic Sea. It is shared by Germany and Poland. Town situated at the Szczecin Lagoon: Świnoujście, Ueckermünde, Wolin (town) and Nowe Warpno.
