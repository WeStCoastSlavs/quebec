Italian cuisine

From the Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia that anyone can change

Spaghetti is traditional Italian cuisine

Italian Cuisine is traditional Italian food. It is often made up of the famous Italian foods such as pasta, pizza, cannoli and red or white wine. Italian cuisine often contains many carbs and sometimes fats. also made up of a lot of vegetables and healthy fats, such as olives, broccoli, and lettuces.
