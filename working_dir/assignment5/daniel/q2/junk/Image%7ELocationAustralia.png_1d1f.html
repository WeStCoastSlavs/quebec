[edit] Summary

World map depicting continent of Australia; differs from map for Commonwealth of Australia

Map includes Australia (including Tasmania and Torres Strait Islands) and New Guinea (including eastern portion of Indonesia (Aru Islands)) and mainland provinces of Papua New Guinea) south/east of Lydekker Line (delimiting continental shelf)

Map adapted from PDF world map at CIA World Fact Book
