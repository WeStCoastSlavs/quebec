Prayer

From the Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia that anyone can change

Prayer is attempting to talk to God or a supernatural being. It is an important part of many religions. There is no conclusive scientific evidence that it is answered. There is no conclusive scientific evidence that it is NOT answered. Prayer is done by those who Believe to the power of word and thought. Almost any Religion recognises the creation of the Universe (by God) out of consciousness = thought = word.
