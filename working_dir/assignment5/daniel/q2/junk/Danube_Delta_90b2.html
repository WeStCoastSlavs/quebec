The Danube Delta (Delta Dunării in Romanian, Дельта Дуная in Russian, Дельта Дунаю in Ukrainian) is the second biggest delta in Europe. It is after the Volga Delta in size. It is one of the best preserved deltas on the continent [1]. Most of the Danube Delta can be found in Romania, but the northern part of the delta is in Ukraine.
