[change] The decade as a whole

This decade will be called "the twenty-thirties". The Roman number will be MMXXX.

[change] Events to happen

  • Voyager 2 will stop transmitting back to Earth.
  • January 19, 2038 - 32-bit clocks will return to 1970.
  • September 1, 2039 will be the hundredth birthday of Nazi Germany's invasion of Poland (the start of World War II).
