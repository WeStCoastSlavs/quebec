Gelsenkirchen is a city in Germany in the state of North Rhine-Westphalia. It is located about 63 km (39.1 mi) north of Cologne in the so called Ruhr Area.

About 269,000 people live there. In the past there were many coal mines. The city is famous for its football (soccer) club FC Schalke 04.
