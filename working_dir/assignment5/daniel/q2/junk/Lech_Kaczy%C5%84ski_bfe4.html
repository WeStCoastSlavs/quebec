Lech Aleksander Kaczyński (born June 18, 1949) has been the President of the Republic of Poland since 2005. Kaczyński served as President of Warsaw from 2002 until the day before he became president. He is the identical twin brother of the Prime Minister of Poland, Jarosław Kaczyński.
