About me


Hi! I'm an administrator here and also at Simple English Wiktionary. You will generally see me doing category cleanup, helping stop vandals, and making sure new pages have correct formatting.

Simple English Wikipedia now has 31,020 articles. Great work everybody!

Nobody's perfect. So if you think I've made a mistake, please let me know.
