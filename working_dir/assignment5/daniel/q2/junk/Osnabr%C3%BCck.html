Osnabrück is a city in Germany in the state of Lower Saxony. It has about 165,000 inhabitants.

Osnabrück is located in south of the state, on the border with North Rhine-Westphalia. It shares an airport with the neighbouring city of Münster which is located in North Rhine-Westphalia.
