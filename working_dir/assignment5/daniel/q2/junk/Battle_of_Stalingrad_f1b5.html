The Battle of Stalingrad is a commonly used name in English sources for several large operations by Germany and its allies and Soviet forces conducted with the purpose of possession of the city of Stalingrad, which took place between 17 July 1942 and February 2 1943, during the Second World War. Stalingrad was known as Tsaritsyn until 1925 and as Volgograd since 1961.
