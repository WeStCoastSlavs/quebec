Appenzell Innerrhoden is one of the twenty-six cantons of Switzerland. It is the smallest canton by population and the second smallest by area.

[change] Districts

The 6 (districts) of Appenzell Innerrhoden are:

  • Appenzell, population 5587 (2002)
  • Gonten, population 1398 (2002)
  • Oberegg, population 1831 (2002)
  • Rüte, population 3036 (2002)
  • Schlatt-Haslen, population 1160 (2002)
  • Schwende, population 2094 (2002)
