[change] Events

  • April 1: Berwick-upon-Tweed is taken by the Scottish from the English.
  • May 11 - Battle of Dysert O'Dea
  • Emperor Go-Daigo becomes king of Japan
  • End of the reign of Emperor Hanazono, emperor of Japan.
  • Qala'un Mosque, Cairo, Egypt started by Al-Nasr Muhammad.

[change] Births

  • June 8 - Eleanor of Woodstock, eldest daughter of King Edward II of England
  • date unknown - Margarete Maultasch, Countess of Tyrol (died 1369)
