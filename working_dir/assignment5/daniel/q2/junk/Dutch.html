The Dutch-speaking world

The Dutch are the people who live in the Netherlands. Often the Netherlands is called Holland, but this is only part of the Netherlands. Dutch is also a language, spoken mostly in the Netherlands, in Belgium, in Suriname and in Netherlands Antilles (a part of the Netherlands). The language spoken in South Africa, called Afrikaans, also looks a lot like the Dutch language.

Many inventors were Dutch, including Hans Lippershey, Hans Janssen, and his son Zacharias Janssen the inventors of the microscope and the telescope, and the incubator. Many famous painters have also been Dutch, among them Rembrandt van Rijn, Vincent van Gogh and Johannes Vermeer.
