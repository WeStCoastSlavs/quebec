Nagasaki

From the Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia that anyone can change

Monument to the Martyrs of Japan, in Nagasaki.

Nagasaki is one of two cities in Japan that was bombed with a nuclear bomb near the end of World War II. The other was Hiroshima.
